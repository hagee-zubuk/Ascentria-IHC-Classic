Fee Management

The Hodges Companies are pleased to offer fee management services. Our clients include residential communities, condominium associations, manufactured housing cooperatives, commercial facilities and low-income housing. Out management services include bookkeeping, collection activity, reporting and accounting. Depending upon your specific needs, we also provide full maintenance services. The Hodges Companies have worked with local lending institutions in Concord and the surrounding areas, as well as the New Hampshire Housing Finance Authority and the New Hampshire division of Rural Development for many years. Through our Fee Management properties, we have also developed relationships with the New Hampshire Community Loan Fund, the Massachusetts division of Rural Development and the Northern New England Investment Fund.
